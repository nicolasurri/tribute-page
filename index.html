Here is a time line of Kobe Bean Bryant's life:

  • 1978: Born in Philadelphia, the youngest of three children and only son of former NBA player Joe Bryant and Pamela Cox Bryant.
  • 1996: Selected 13th in the 1996 NBA Draft by the Charlotte Hornets and traded to the Los Angeles Lakers.
  • 2000: NBA champion, the first of a "three-peat"
  • 2001: Gets married with Vanessa Laine, who then became the mother of his childs. Second NBA championship.
  • 2002: Champion of the NBA for the thrid time.
  • 2003: Birth of his first daughter, Natalia Bryant.
  • 2006: Birth of his second daughter, Gigi Bryant.
  • 2009 & 2010: The last two NBA championships for Kobe.
  • 2016: Vanessa gave birth to their third daughter, Bianka Bryant. Kobe retires with a 60-point season-high in his last NBA game.
  • 2018: Wins the Oscar "Best Animated Short Film" with "Dear Basketball"
  • 2019: Birth of his fourth daughter, Capri Bryant.
  • 2020: Dies in helicopter crash accident together with her daughter Gigi Bryant.
